## 🎯 Goal

You are ready for the exam if:

- You consistently score **80%+**
- You understand *why* answers are correct
- You can distinguish similar services quickly

---

## 💡 Tip

In the exam:
- Read questions carefully
- Look for keywords like:
  - *best*
  - *least effort*
  - *most cost-effective*

👉 These often determine the correct answer.
